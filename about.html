About Us

Welcome To DeCafe Resturant!

DeCafe is a beautiful, spacious location where visitors can enjoy the signature taste they have come to expect from us in a sophisticated yet relaxed dining atmosphere. DeCafe Restaurant, established in the heart of karachi since 2006, originally opened in Lahore in 1966, is foremost amongst Pakistani restaurants. Beautifully situated in Clifton Karachi, close to the Berkeley Hotel and just down the road from Harrods. We are here to serve you amazing food, give you the best memories and make your day better.
